If you are pondering over the question baccarat how to play on the Internet? We are at your disposal with the easy-to-understand explanation of its rules.

    According to baccarat rules, which are almost identical for the online and the live play, six or eight standard English 52-card decks are used here. There is a dealer, who deals cars to players. Your task is to make a bet and take cards. Three betting options are available in baccarat. You can wager on the Banker’s hand, the Player’s hand or the Tie. Let us have a closer look at baccarat game rules now.

    Place Your Bet and Choose the Hand

    When you start the play, you will see a table, where the game action takes place. Your first step is to place your bet. Then you will get the Player’s hand consisting of two cards and the dealer will get the Banker’s hand also with two cards. All cards then will be revealed and you will be able to count their value. Cards from 2 to 9 have their actual value represented by their numbers (from 2 to 9); 10, Jack, Queen and King cards count as zero and an Ace card is 1. For example, if you have 3+9=12, your hand’s value will be 2 ( the last digit indicates it). The hand with the higher value will be the winning one. On the contrary, if you choose the Tie bet, you hope that two hands (the Player’s and the Banker’s one) will have the equal value. There are also special rules, which explain when the Player’s or the Banker’s hand can get the third card. For example, if the Player’s hand value is 5 or less, you will get the third card. However, there are some nuances, which can be useful. Every player should know that the baccarat house edge is one of the lowest ones among online casino games. it is 1.06% and it concerns the Banker’s bet. The Player’s bet has 1.24% and the Tie’s bet house edge is 14.36%. All this means that the most beneficial decision of a gambler in this game is to make bets on the Banker’s hand.
