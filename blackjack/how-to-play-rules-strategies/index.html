    Here are the blackjack tips concerning the cards splitting.

    Doubling down strategy

    Here are the tips concerning the doubling down meaning doubling your bet in exchange for getting one more card.

    • Always double down if you have hard 9, 10 or 11 (this is why knowing about hard and soft hands is important);
    • Always double down if you have 11 except for the cases when the dealer has an Ace;
