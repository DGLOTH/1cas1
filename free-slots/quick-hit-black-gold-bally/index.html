    Quick Hit Black Gold Game Review

    Do not be deceived by the dated presentation. Quick Hit Black Gold is an excellent game that combines a retro look with a fun set of gameplay and bonus features. Give it a try, for you may just come to like it.

    Black Gold is a classic slot made by Bally Technologies. All 4 slots have a 3×5 layout, but you can say that Black Gold and Quick Hit Pro are more of the classic slots, while Platinum and Las Vegas are a bit more edgy. You can make bet in a range from 1 cent to 15 euros that means 450 euros per round.

    The symbols you will see on Quick Hits Black Gold slot are familiar to any casino player: 7s, Bars, Cherries, Bells and others. The special symbols are Free Games; Quick Hit logo which is the scatter multiplier. The Locking Wilds is the best feature of Quick Hits Black Gold slot and can be truly rewarding. Each Locking Wild that appears on the reels is locked into position until the end of the feature. Additional Quick Hit Scatter symbols are added on the reels, which allows you to get up to 11 of them on the reels. For 9 you will get x2500 your total bet, 10 worth x5000 and 11 will get you x7500 your total bet.

    As we mentioned before, Black Gold has 4 Free Spins features. To trigger any of them, you will need to get 3 Free Games Fever symbols on reels 2, 3 and 4. That will get you 10 free spins. Then, you will need to select from a grid of 18 tiles until you match three similar symbols – that will define which of the 4 Free Games features you won.

    Black Gold is another part of Bally’s successful Quick Hit slots series. You can play online for fun or for real money. You should notice that Black Gold is a high variance game, which means you can have a relatively long losing streak and should be a bit careful when playing this slot for real money. However when it pays, it pays well.
