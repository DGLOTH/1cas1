    Bonus Deluxe Game Review

    Bonus Deluxe is a video poker game by Betsoft which features a unusual type of gameplay with three separate paytables and looks a bit outdated in terms of design. The game is hard to describe from the first glance, so let’s take a deeper look.

    This particular free video poker is played with a standard deck and has no wild cards. The three paytables may seem weird but they work utterly simple. The right one will qualify whenever the three first cards from right to left make up a winning hand. The left table works vice versa. What about the five card paytable, it works as an usual one. Each paytable plays an independent bet for each deal, so when you wager five, you basically deal 15. By the word, the betting range varies between 1 and 5. The cards are not held automatically, so you need to do it yourself, and it is great because video poker games are way too simple nowadays. The RNG is not satisfying, to be honest, because the losing streaks are just boring sometimes.

The card should have a higher value than the dealer’s one. The number of attempts is unlimited unless you lose or reach the casino limit. Not the best bonus feature ever, but it is implemented into nearly each video poker.

    To conclude, Bonus Deluxe by Betsoft is easy to play pyramid type video poker which has a conventional design and has a lack of balanced RNG, however, it is interesting to try the three paytables simultaneously. No download is required, so you can try it right away.
