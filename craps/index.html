    Rules and How to Play Craps Online

    In general, craps rules of a traditional and of free demo dice game do not differ and are quite easy. In this dice casino game there is one player, who throws dice and s/he is called a shooter. Other players near the table should guess the number which will show these two dice (in other words, they bet). The first roll is called the come-out throw and is very important for the future game. Before this come-out throw, a player should bet the Pass Line or the Don’t Pass Bar. The Pass Line bet wins when dice show 7 or 11 in sum. The Don’t Pass Bar wins when players see 2, 3 or 12 on the dice (another name of these combinations is ‘craps’). In case there is any other number on the table, it becomes the ‘Point’. The game goes on with all possible bets according to its rules.
