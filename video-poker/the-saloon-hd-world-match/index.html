    The Saloon HD Game Review

    The Saloon HD by World Match is another decent example of video poker made with the beloved HD feature and a great western like design. Whether this game deserves a good mark or not, let’s take a closer look.

    This particular free video poker is played with a standard deck and has no wild cards in it. The betting range varies between 0.1 and 5. The paytable is balanced according to the betting range and the maximum payoff is 1000 for the Royal Flush. The minimal winning hand is Jacks or Better.

    The game will automatically suggest which cards to hold in order to draw a good hand, but this system is not perfect. Otherwise, it wouldn’t be necessary to even have a player staring at the monitor. The RNG is satisfying enough, but it could better because of comparatively low payoffs. Your main goal here is just to build the strongest hand possible. The RTP is better at the highest wager.

    The bonus round doesn’t differ much from the other video poker games in terms of the concept. You are simply offered to double up your winnings or lose the. The peculiarity of this particular gambling round is that you need to guess the number on the Magic Dice. The options offer to pick the even or an odd number or to pick the last or first three numbers.

    The Saloon HD by World Match is overall a great game in comparison to other online casino games. It features pleasant graphics, high definition, and satisfying RNG. On the other hand, the range of possible decisions makes this game too simple. The verdict is that this one may be the best to start, but not anyhow interesting for an experienced gambler.
